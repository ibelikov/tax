# Dutch BV · DGA Tax Model (2026)

An interactive, single-page calculator for the corporate and personal tax burden of a
Dutch **BV** with a single shareholder/director (**DGA**), for **tax year 2026**.

Change the inputs — hourly rate, billable days, salary, dividend — and every figure
(VPB, Box 1, Zvw, Box 2, BTW, take-home, effective rates) recalculates live.

**Live:** https://ibelikov.github.io/tax/

## Use it

Open `index.html` in any browser. That's the whole app — one self-contained static
file, no build step, no dependencies, no backend. Nothing you type leaves your browser.

## Notes

- All 2026 rates and thresholds are editable in the **2026 tax parameters** panel.
- Light/dark theme follows your system preference and remembers your choice.
- This is a **planning model, not tax advice**. It ignores Box 3, the 30% ruling,
  pension build-up, prior-year retained earnings, partner/income-splitting and the
  gebruikelijkloon comparison test. Confirm with a Dutch accountant before acting.

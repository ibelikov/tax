Every rate and threshold the model reads. Edit any cell to test a different year or scenario — the whole page recalculates.

This is a planning model, not tax advice. It assumes a single shareholder/DGA below AOW age, a Dec 31 year-end, and the low Zvw "bijdrage" rate (4.85%) withheld from the DGA. It ignores Box 3, the 30% ruling, mortgage interest, pension build-up, prior-year retained earnings, partner/income-splitting and the gebruikelijkloon comparison test. The €58,000 customary-salary minimum is informational only. Confirm the structure and the Zvw treatment with a Dutch accountant before acting.
Sources: Belastingdienst (voorlopige aanslag 2026; dividendbelasting; VPB), KVK 2026 rates, Salaris Vanmorgen (Zvw 2026), De Zaak (Box 2 2026).
